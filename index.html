How to play the game:

The goal of the game is to earn as much money as possible by completing customer orders.

1) Click "Start Game" to start the game
2) Look at the selection of items ordered by the customer and drag the items to the tray.
3) Click "enter order" when the order is ready.
Each correct item will add $10 to the score, each wrong item will decrease the score with $20.
Good luck!
